SAINTGITS COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

Saintgits Institute of Computer Engineering and Technology Applications (SICETA) offers complete professional grooming for students to a successful career in the IT industry and provides value-based education through a system of wholesome learning. It focuses on enhancing education and research by bringing together students, teachers, and academicians from around the world to create an opportunity for the exchange of knowledge and ideas. SICETA has professional researchers and well-trained faculty members whose teaching transform minds and facilitate the students to come up with innovative ideas. SICETA offers following full-time under graduate, post graduate and research programmes.
