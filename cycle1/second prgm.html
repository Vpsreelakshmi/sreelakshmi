MASTER OF COMPUTER APPLICATION

The Master of Computer Application (MCA) degree programme is an advancement over the BCA course. It is ideal for students who want to pursue a career in the IT sector. Students are trained to have a sound foundation in the fundamentals of computer technology, a high level of practical skill in the use of that technology and at the same time to be sensitive to the issues prevailing in the society. The alumni of the MCA department are well placed in top positions of the major IT companies in India and abroad. The course emphasis on latest programming languages and tools to develop better and faster applications. BCA makes a base while MCA adds a specialized touch in the subjects studied in BCA.The duration of MCA course is total three academic years. It is a full-time postgraduate course offered by many colleges/Universities situated in India. This course has been divided into six semesters with the duration of six months each. The first year of this course focuses on the skill developments in computers, second year is aimed for providing a conceptual framework and the third year offers the specializations and project work.
